Bio

Being a DJ has lead me to becoming an electronic music producer and I use various different mixing techniques, analogue synthesizers and software to produce sounds and music.

Finding the right tone, tweeking knobs, pressing buttons or the slide of a mouse to get the right amount of modulation or sythnesis is what gets me out of bed in the morning. Creating all types of different sounds in different ways can be a fascinating journey of sonic discovery.

For my final college project I was assigned a scene taken for the Unity store which was free to use.
The scene had no sound so the brief was to interpret the scene and add sounds accordingly.
All the sounds are either sounds I created using synthesis or foley sounds that I recorded.
